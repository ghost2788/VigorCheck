Account Deletion

Request deletion of your VigorCheck account.

Use this page to request deletion of your VigorCheck account and associated app data. Requests are handled by VigorCheck LLC.

Last updated: April 21, 2026

How to request deletion

Email loothunt.info@gmail.com from the Google account email you use with VigorCheck, or include that email address in your request so we can identify the account.

What will be deleted

We will delete or anonymize account profile data, nutrition plan data, reminders, meal and hydration logs, scan history, and other VigorCheck app data associated with your account unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, billing, or dispute-resolution reasons.

Subscriptions

Account deletion does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play subscription settings: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions .
