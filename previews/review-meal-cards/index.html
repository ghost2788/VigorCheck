Review meal polish, phase 2

Three ways to move meal type into the card instead of the separate top strip.

All three options keep the history-aligned shell, the tighter nutrition stack, and the no-duplicate-title rule. The only variable here is how meal type lives near prep method and the other review-only controls.

Theme

What changed

  • The separate meal-type card is gone in every option.
  • Meal type now lives inside the expanded review card, near prep method.
  • The top of the screen gets cleaner and the edit controls read more like one settings zone.

What to compare

  • Does meal type feel like a setting or like a classification badge?
  • Do you want direct pill access, or a cleaner summary row first?
