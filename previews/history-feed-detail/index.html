CalTracker history concept

Recent-day history feed with a day detail timeline and direct edit access.

This keeps Trends analytics-focused and gives logging a proper audit trail. The feed is simple, the day view is chronological, and meal plus hydration edits stay explicit.

Theme

History feed

  • One card per day, newest first.
  • Daily wellness stays visible, but secondary to the day’s logs.
  • Infinite scroll is implied, not called out.

Day detail

  • Meals and hydration share one descending timeline.
  • Each row exposes edit and delete immediately.
  • FoodScan, Text entry, and manual stay visible as source badges.
