Favorites add-feedback ideas

All options keep the tapped favorite in the same position. The only change after tapping + is confirmation feedback, not resorting.

Recommendation

I’d recommend Option A: keep the row order frozen, morph the plus button into a success check briefly, and show a small confirmation pill near the section header. It reads clearly, feels modern, and does not add much visual weight.

  • Keep audio off by default. A subtle haptic tap is the better mobile cue here.
  • Favorites should behave like pinned shortcuts, not like a “most recently used” list.
  • Recently added can still refresh independently without disturbing the favorites order.

Option A: Check-button + top confirmation pill

Cleanest version. The row stays put, the tapped plus button becomes a green check for a beat, and a compact “Added to today” pill appears above the favorites stack.
