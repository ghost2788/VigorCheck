The flagship hero should follow accent tuning

The welcome and paywall hero currently bakes in one gold. The proposal keeps the same silhouette and branded feeling but moves rails, glow, and brackets onto palette-driven accent layers.

Before fixed gold constants
Glow and frame stay the same even if the design system adjusts the accent palette.
After accent2-driven frame and glow
The branded silhouette stays intact, but the component now moves with the active accent palette in dark and light mode.
Approval intent
